Build your first app

To build a basic app with Web AppBuilder, complete the following steps:

  1. Start Web AppBuilder.
  2. Log in to your portal (ArcGIS Online Organization or Portal for ArcGIS).
  3. On the Web AppBuilder home page, click Create New.
  4. Choose Default (2D), provide the app name and description, and click OK.
  5. Accept the default theme, map, and widgets.
  6. Click the Attribute tab, change the title, subtitle, and logo of the app.
  7. Click Save to save your changes.
  8. Click Previews to view the app in your devices.
  9. Click Launch to view the app in your browser.
  10. Click the Web AppBuilder icon or Web AppBuilder for ArcGIS at the upper left of your browser to return to the home page.
  11. Hover over to the Download button on your app and click it to download the app for deployment.
