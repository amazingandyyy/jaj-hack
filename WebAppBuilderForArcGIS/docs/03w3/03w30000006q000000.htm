Zoom Slider widget

The Zoom Slider widget provides interactive zoom controls in the map display.

Configuring the Zoom Slider widget

Steps:
  1. Hover over the Zoom Slider widget and click the small eye icon to show or hide this widget on the map.
  2. No configuration is needed.

Using the Zoom Slider widget

Click the plus or minus button to zoom in or out on the map.
